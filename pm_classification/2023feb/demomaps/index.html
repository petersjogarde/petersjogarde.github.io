Maps of biomedical sciences using citation-based classification

This page shows some example maps based on citation clusters in PubMed resulting from my PhD project "Mapping the structure of science through clustering in citation networks : granularity, labeling and visualization".
The classification of about 20 million publications in PubMed is available in figshare. See the thesis and figshare collection for further information.
